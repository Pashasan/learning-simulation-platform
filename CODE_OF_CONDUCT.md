# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want this project to be a welcoming place to learn and contribute, regardless of background, experience level, or identity. As contributors and maintainers, we pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

This applies to issues, pull requests, discussions, code review comments, and any other interaction tied to the project.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community and the people learning from this material
- Showing empathy toward other community members
- Crediting prior work when you build on someone else's contribution

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Personal attacks, insulting comments, or trolling
- Public or private harassment of any kind
- Publishing others' private information (physical address, email, etc.) without explicit permission
- Sexualized language, imagery, or unwanted attention of a sexual nature
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional or educational setting

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces — the repository, issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other channels associated with the project — and when an individual is representing the project in public spaces.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported privately to the project maintainer through any of these channels:

- Open a private vulnerability advisory: [https://github.com/Pashasan/learning-simulation-platform/security/advisories/new](https://github.com/Pashasan/learning-simulation-platform/security/advisories/new) — this works for conduct reports too and creates a private channel between you and the maintainer
- Contact the maintainer through their [GitHub profile](https://github.com/Pashasan)

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. The maintainer is obligated to maintain confidentiality with regard to the reporter of an incident.

Maintainers who do not follow or enforce this Code of Conduct in good faith may face temporary or permanent repercussions as determined by other members of the project's leadership.

## Consequences

Project maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate, including warning, temporary ban, or permanent ban from project spaces, for behavior that violates this Code of Conduct.
