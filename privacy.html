Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 14, 2026

Overview

Generative Education Platform ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our educational gaming platform.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address, username, and password (stored securely using industry-standard encryption).
  • Game Data: We collect your game scores, progress, badges earned, and performance statistics to track your learning journey and display on leaderboards.
  • Feedback: If you choose to provide feedback after completing a game, we collect your ratings and comments to improve our platform.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain your account
  • Track your learning progress across game volumes
  • Display your scores on public leaderboards (using your chosen display name)
  • Improve our educational content based on aggregate performance data and feedback
  • Conduct educational research and learning analytics studies (using anonymized data)
  • Communicate important updates about the platform

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud database provider. We implement appropriate security measures including:

  • Encrypted password storage
  • Secure HTTPS connections
  • Row-level security policies to protect your data

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your information may be visible to other users only in the following ways:

  • Leaderboards: Your display name and scores are visible on public leaderboards

We may share anonymized, aggregate data for educational research purposes.

Data Use for Research

Anonymized gameplay data may be used for educational research purposes, including:

  • Learning pattern analysis: Understanding how students interact with different question types, topics, and difficulty levels
  • Educational effectiveness: Measuring learning outcomes and identifying areas for improvement in our content
  • Academic publications: Contributing to educational technology research through published studies using anonymized, aggregate data

All research data is stripped of personally identifiable information. Your email, display name, and any information that could identify you individually are removed before analysis. You may opt out of research use at any time (see Your Rights below).

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data (self-service via Settings page or by contacting us)
  • Opt out of having your anonymized data used for research purposes
  • Choose not to provide optional feedback

Cookies and Local Storage

We use browser local storage and session storage to maintain your login session. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

Children's Privacy

Our platform is designed for educational purposes and may be used by learners of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your rights regarding your personal data, please contact us through the platform or by opening an issue on our GitHub repository.
